## Overview

Cards provide a flexible container for summarizing entities such as projects, resources, or announcements. They can include imagery, supporting text, and action buttons. Keep the content hierarchy consistent: header, body, then footer.

When cards act as navigation triggers, mark the `interactive` flag and ensure the entire card is focusable. Include descriptive text that conveys the destination or action taken.

## UX Guidelines

- Maintain consistent padding and typography across card variants to reinforce familiarity.
- Align card actions horizontally and prefer primary buttons on the right for left-to-right locales.
- Use the subtle variant on surfaces that already provide elevation to avoid competing shadows.

## Accessibility details

Interactive cards must provide a clear focus state and respond to both click and keyboard activation (Enter/Space).
